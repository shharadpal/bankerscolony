❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription? ▾
Log in → Dashboard → Subscription → Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. For a refund, contact us within 7 days of payment.
I paid but my account still shows Free plan ▾
Log out and log back in — it usually resolves in 15 minutes. If not, contact us with your Razorpay Payment ID (starts with "pay_") and we will fix it within 2 hours.
How do I report a wrong answer in an MCQ? ▾
Use this form with category "MCQ / Content". Mention the MCQ ID (shown as #BA-001 on the practice page) and explain why the answer is incorrect with a reference.
Can I get a refund? ▾
Yes. We offer a full 7-day refund guarantee on first subscription payments. Contact us with your payment details. See our Refund Policy for full details.
