Our Mission

Bankers Colony was founded with one purpose — to give every Indian banker and banking aspirant access to the highest quality exam preparation material at a price that does not burn a hole in their pocket.

We saw the gap. Good study material for JAIIB, CAIIB and bank promotion exams was either expensive, scattered, or outdated. So we built the platform we wished had existed — every MCQ, every PDF note, every mock test crafted by bankers with first-hand exam experience.
