🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 Jan 2026 · Last Updated: 1 May 2026
Plain Language SummaryWe collect your name, email, and usage data to run the platform. We never sell your data. Payments are handled by Razorpay — we never see your card details. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Who We Are

BankersColony.com is an online banking exam preparation platform operated from India. For privacy queries: privacy@bankerscolony.com

2. Information We Collect

Information You Give Us

  • Registration: Name, email address, mobile number, exam category
  • Profile: Any additional information you add to your profile
  • Contact Form: Name, email, and message when you contact us
  • Payment: Plan selection only. Actual payment details are handled by Razorpay — we never see your card, UPI ID or bank account details

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: MCQs attempted, tests taken, scores, time spent
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, device type
  • Cookies: Session cookies to keep you logged in. Google Analytics cookies (anonymised)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To deliver the services you have subscribed to
  • To send exam alerts, banking news, and educational content you opted into
  • To send transactional emails (verification, receipts, password reset)
  • To improve our platform through anonymised usage analysis

4. Advertising — Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing (name and email only)
  • Google — Analytics (anonymised) and AdSense
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — anonymised prompts for daily awareness content. No personal user data is shared.
  • Legal Authorities — if required by Indian law or court order

6. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages (SSL/TLS)
  • Bcrypt password hashing — plain-text passwords are never stored
  • Private AWS S3 storage with signed URLs for PDF access

7. Your Rights Under Indian Law

Under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and IT Rules 2011, you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data. Email privacy@bankerscolony.com to exercise these rights.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data while your account is active. On deletion request, personal data is removed within 30 days (except payment records required by Indian GST law for 7 years).

9. Children's Privacy

Bankers Colony is intended for users 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be emailed to registered users. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
